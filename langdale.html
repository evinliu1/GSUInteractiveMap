Langdale Hall is currently named after former university president Noah Langdale Jr. who, along with the Board of Regents, in 1959 ignored the ruling of the District Court in Hunt v. Arnold and did not allow desegregation at Georgia State. During Noah Langdale's 31 years as president, the Georgia State College of Business Administration grew to become Georgia State University.Funding for Georgia State increased from a budge of $1.9 million to $118.6 million under Langdale's leadership.
