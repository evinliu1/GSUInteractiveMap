The student recreation center opened in August 2001 and in 2012 became Green Globes certified. It earn 3/4 gobles due to the commitment to green building and sustainable developement. In 2002 it was recognized by NIRSA as an outstanding sport facility. The center is offer to students to have a stress free environment and to relax after studying. The center offer a free-weight area, spa, sauna, training room and 35 foot climbing wall.
